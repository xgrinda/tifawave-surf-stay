# Tifawave Media & Content Quality Checklist

Use this before publishing room or gallery images.

## Image Sizing

- Room primary images: 2400 x 1600 px, landscape, WebP or JPG.
- Room gallery images: 1800 x 1200 px minimum, consistent color tone.
- Public gallery images: 1800 px wide minimum; mix landscape and portrait crops.
- Hero or wide editorial images: 2400 px wide minimum.
- Keep files below 500 KB when practical; use compression before adding URLs.
- Admin uploads accept JPG, PNG, WebP, and AVIF up to 5 MB.

## Content Quality

- Choose bright, inspectable images of real rooms, rooftops, surf, food, and Tamraght.
- Avoid dark, blurry, heavily filtered, or overly cropped images.
- Make the first room image the clearest view of the actual room.
- Keep captions short and specific, for example "Sunset from the rooftop".
- Use category tags consistently, for example `surf`, `rooms`, `food`, `tamraght`, `community`.

## Alt Text

- Describe what is visible, not marketing copy.
- Include useful context when it matters: room name, activity, or location.
- Keep alt text under 180 characters.
- Good: "Private double room with linen bedding and morning light".
- Avoid: "Amazing beautiful luxury room image".

## URL Checklist

- Use a direct `https://` image URL.
- Confirm the URL opens the image without a login.
- Avoid temporary signed URLs that expire.
- For admin uploads, confirm the `tifawave-media` Supabase Storage bucket exists.
- Replace broken URLs immediately; public pages will show a fallback visual.
- Do not upload guest-identifiable images without permission.
